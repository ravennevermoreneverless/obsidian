### 英訳えいやく	

I think that...
### 文型ぶんけい

a. short-pastと思おもう = I think...
b. short-neg-pastと思おもう = I don't think...
### 例文れいぶん



### ノート

As in the present tense, you use the short form in the past tense with ～と思おもいます (I think) to report what you think took place in the past.
