— wash chicken
— cut into cubes
— add 200:30 ratio of flour and cornstarch
— add 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
— add paprika
— add salt and pepper

— mix until coated
— deep fry chicken cubes
— taste as u go
— double fry (optional)
